Take a break from the pastures and head over to The Silo Café, our rustic corner of the farm dedicated to honest, home-cooked flavors. Everything on our menu starts with a harvest; whether it’s the heirloom tomatoes in our signature "Farmer’s Melt" or the fresh-picked berries in our famous sourdough scones, you’re tasting the very land you just walked on. Grab a stool at the reclaimed wood counter, enjoy a mug of our locally roasted "Dawn Patrol" coffee, and watch the goats play through our floor-to-ceiling barn windows. It’s not just a meal—it’s a front-row seat to farm life.

Farmhouse Breakfast

The Early Bird Wrap – Farm-fresh scrambled eggs, sautéed kale, sharp white cheddar, and maple sausage in a toasted flour tortilla.
Llama Pancakes – Three fluffy buttermilk pancakes shaped like our favorite farm friends, served with whipped honey butter and real property-tapped maple syrup.
Steel-Cut Harvest Oats – Slow-cooked oats topped with toasted walnuts, flax seeds, and a seasonal berry compote.

Garden-Fresh Lunch
Available 11:30 AM – 3:00 PM
