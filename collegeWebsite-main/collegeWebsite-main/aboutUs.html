Welcome to Green Acres Farm
If you have questions about your visit or want to book a private event, getting in touch with our team is as easy as a summer breeze. You can reach the main farm office by phone at (555) 012-FARM during our standard operating hours, or drop us an email at hello@greenacresfarm.com for general inquiries and group tour rates.

Meet Farmer Ben, our Head of Operations and resident "Llama Whisperer." With over fifteen years of experience, Ben oversees everything from orchard health to livestock care.

Sarah is the culinary force behind The Silo Café and our resident "Sourdough Queen." With a background in pastry arts, she transforms our harvest into seasonal treats.

Marcus is our Lead Ranger and the primary caretaker of the Biggar Nature Trails. A former wilderness scout, he knows every hidden overlook across our fifty acres.

To see what’s happening on the farm in real-time—including newborn animal announcements and daily café specials—be sure to follow us on Instagram and Facebook @GreenAcresFarm. We love seeing your photos, so don't forget to tag us or use the hashtag #GreenAcresLife for a chance to be featured on our community board. For those already on the property, our staff members are easily identifiable by their green farm vests and are always happy to help you find a trail or identify a specific breed of sheep!
